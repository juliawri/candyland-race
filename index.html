Candy Land Race

Answer trivia questions to race to Candy Castle! Right answers move you forward, wrong answers send you back. Your score rewards speed and accuracy.

📜🍬

How to Play

Answer each trivia question by picking one of the 4 options. Get it right and you hop 1 space forward. Get it wrong and you slide 1 space back.

Race solo — there are no computer opponents. Just you against the trail!

First to reach 🏰 Candy Castle wins. Your final score combines how fast you finished and how many questions it took — fewer questions and less time means a higher score.

⚠️ If you run out of trivia questions before reaching the castle, your score is 0 — so answer carefully!
