📜🍬

How to Play

Tap Draw Card to reveal a candy colour. Your piece hops forward to the next space of that colour. Then each computer racer takes its turn automatically.

Watch out for special spaces: 🍫 Molasses Swamp makes you skip your next turn, and 🍭 Gumdrop Pass zooms you forward!

First to reach 🏰 Candy Castle wins. If you win, your turn count is saved to the leaderboard — fewer turns is better!
